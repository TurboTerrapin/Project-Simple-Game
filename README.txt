A and D, along with the horizontal arrow keys will move the character left or right.

Space will make the character jump, you can hold space to make the character jump higher.

Enemies will attempt to move either left or right, although they often get stuck.

You can break the brown bricks by jumping into them.

If you try the same thing with the golden blocks, they will spawn an upgrade.

Upgrades will provide you with a small boost to lives, speed, or jump height

Jump into the spinning cube at the end of the level to start the next level. There are only two levels.
